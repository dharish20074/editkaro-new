Edit Karo.
It Started As Advice.

Before it was an agency, "edit karo" was just what we kept telling friends with great footage and terrible pacing. Eventually we started doing it for them.

Mission

To make every brand and creator's footage look like it had a team twice their size behind it — without the overhead of actually building one in-house.

Vision

A timeline where any business in India — from a five-table café to a fifty-person D2C team — can post video that looks like it belongs on the biggest screen in the room.

How we work

Four rules
we don't break.

PRINCIPLE

We cut for the platform, not the camera.

A Reel and a YouTube video are never the same edit, even from the same footage.

PRINCIPLE

Color is a decision, not a filter.

Every grade is built for the brand's palette — never a one-click preset.

PRINCIPLE

Deadlines are timecodes, not suggestions.

If we said Thursday 6 PM, your export lands Thursday 6 PM.

PRINCIPLE

We ship finished files, not loose ends.

Every deliverable arrives captioned, graded, and sized — ready to post, not to finish.
